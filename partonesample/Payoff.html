Your Payoff

After you have completed all 20 rounds, the computer will randomly select one out of the 20 auctions, with each auction being equally likely to be selected. Your total earnings in the selected auction will be the sum of your earnings from your guess about the highest of the other three bids (Part I) and your earnings from bidding in the auction (Part II). You should therefore in each of the 20 auctions choose your guess and bid carefully as if it were the ones that will determine your payoff.

Your Payment in the Experiment

At the end of the experiment your payoff in credits (C) will be converted into dollars with an exchange rate of $1 for C6. In addition, you will receive a participation fee of $10 and an endowment of $12. Negative earnings (losses) will be subtracted from your endowment of $12.

Hence, your payment will be calculated as follows:

Payment = $10 participation fee + $12 endowment + Payoff in Auction (in $)
